For those who seek to be 'industry ready'

The STEM Leaders Program is our premier STEM development program initiative. We provide you with the opportunity to develop your technical and soft skills in a challenging and supportive environment through the completion of an industry project and various professional development workshop.

Program Structure

The STEM Leaders Program runs over four weeks during the trimester break and is designed to take you out of your comfort zone, providing you with a platform to develop yourself both personally and professionally.

Phase 1 - Humble Beginnings

An introduction to new concepts - setting the foundations for a growth mindset in preparation for the new work order.

Phase 2 - Taking Flight

Cultivate organic networking skills to engage with industry professionals

Phase 3 - Airborne

Reinforcing your presentation skills with a new sense of profound confidence and expanding your technical abilities by working on cutting edge technologies.

Phase 4 - Conquering the Skies

Showcase your project solution to industry partners and reflecting on your development to go above and beyond.
